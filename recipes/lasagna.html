Lasagna

How do we make this delicious looking lasagna?

To be really honest, it is a lot of work.

Alternatively, you can just buy it from Aldi. They are pretty good.

We'll need some ingredients!

  • Meat: 1/2 pound of ground beef and 1/2 pound of ground pork;
  • Onion: 1 diced onion;
  • Canned tomatoes: 1 can of tomato sauce and a can of crushed tomatoes;
  • Fresh herbs: 2 tbs of parsley and 1 clove of garlic;
  • Sugar: A dash will suffice;
  • Spices and seasonings: Dried basil, dried oregano, salt and black pepper to taste;
  • Noodles: Some lasagna noodles;
  • Cheese: Cottage cheese, parmesan and shredded mozzarella;
  • Eggs: A LOT OF EGGS because they are yummy.

How do we do this?

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the meat in something that doesn't melt and stop cooking before it looks black;
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente;
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix as much cheese as you can stomach with your 23 eggs and gathered herbs in a bowl;
  4. Assembling time!: Put stuff on top of each other untill it looks like a lasagna;
  5. Bake the lasagna!: Cover with foil and bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes at 375 degrees F.
