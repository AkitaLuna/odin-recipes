Pancakes

How do we make these delicious looking pancakes?

To be really honest, it is a lot of work.

Alternatively, you can just buy them from Aldi. They are pretty good.

We'll need some ingredients!

  • Flour: All-purpose flour;
  • Baking powder: One of these to make them extra fluffy!;
  • Sugar: Nothing sweeter than a tbs of sugar;
  • Salt: Not too much ofcourse;
  • Sugar: A dash will suffice;
  • Milk and butter: Get them moist and rich;
  • Eggs: A LOT OF EGGS because they are yummy.

How do we do this?

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together;
  2. Make a well and add the wet ingredients. Don't forget to stir!;
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan;
  4. Cook for a few minutes and flip;
  5. Continue cooking till they are brown, not black.
